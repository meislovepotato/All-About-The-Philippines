Philippine Presidents

1899-1901

1946-1948

Elpidio Quirino

"Magsaysay"

1957-1961

"Filipino First"

"New Society" and "Bagong Lipunan" (New Society)

Corazon Aquino

1986-1992

"People Power" and "Tama Na, Sobra Na, Palitan Na" (Enough, Excesses, Change)

Fidel V. Ramos

1992-1998

"Philippines 2000" and "Pilipino Muna" (Filipinos First)

Joseph Estrada

1998-2001

"Erap para sa Mahirap" (Erap for the Poor)

Gloria Macapagal-Arroyo

"Strong Republic" and "One Philippines"
