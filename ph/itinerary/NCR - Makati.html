NCR - Makati Itinerary

Itinerary 1: 

Day 1: Makati City Exploration

Morning: Start your day by exploring Ayala Triangle Gardens in Makati. Take a leisurely walk or jog in this green oasis and enjoy the serene surroundings.

Visit Ayala Museum, which is located nearby. Discover the rich history and cultural heritage of the Philippines through its exhibits and galleries.

Afternoon:Head to Greenbelt Mall in Makati. This upscale shopping complex offers a wide range of local and international brands, as well as various dining options. Enjoy a delicious lunch at one of the restaurants in the mall.

Explore the Ayala Museum complex further by visiting the Filipinas Heritage Library. Browse through their collection of books, documents, and photographs that delve deeper into Philippine history and culture.

Evening: Visit the vibrant Poblacion neighborhood in Makati, known for its bustling nightlife. Explore the diverse range of bars, pubs, and restaurants in the area. You can have dinner at one of the trendy establishments or try street food from the local vendors.

Day 2: Explore Nearby Cities

Morning: Visit Bonifacio Global City (BGC) in Taguig, a neighboring city of Makati. Explore the High Street, where you can shop for local and international brands, dine at various restaurants, or relax in one of the cafes.

Explore The Mind Museum in BGC, a science museum that offers interactive exhibits and educational experiences. Learn about different scientific concepts and engage in hands-on activities.

Afternoon: Head to Pasay City and visit the Cultural Center of the Philippines (CCP). Marvel at the contemporary and traditional arts showcased in its galleries and watch performances at the CCP Complex.

Explore the Mall of Asia (MOA) complex in Pasay. It is one of the largest malls in Asia, offering a wide range of retail shops, restaurants, and entertainment options. You can enjoy shopping, dining, or even catch a movie.

Evening: Visit the Entertainment City in Para√±aque, known for its casinos and entertainment venues. Experience the vibrant atmosphere and try your luck at one of the casinos or enjoy a live performance.
Enjoy dinner at a restaurant in the Entertainment City complex, which offers a variety of cuisines from local to international.

Day 3: Cultural and Historical Sites

Morning: Visit Intramuros, the historic walled city in Manila. Explore Fort Santiago, a Spanish-era fortress that played a significant role in Philippine history. Walk along the cobblestone streets and visit landmarks such as Manila Cathedral and San Agustin Church.

Take a kalesa (horse-drawn carriage) ride within Intramuros to experience a nostalgic mode of transportation.

Afternoon: Visit the National Museum of the Philippines in Manila. Explore its extensive collection of art, historical artifacts, and cultural exhibits that showcase the heritage of the Philippines.
Explore the nearby Rizal Park (Luneta), a historic park where you can relax, enjoy a picnic, or visit the monument of national hero Jose Rizal.

Evening: End your trip with a visit to the Mall of Asia (MOA) in Pasay. Enjoy a scenic sunset view by the Manila Baywalk and indulge in a delicious dinner at one of the waterfront restaurants.
