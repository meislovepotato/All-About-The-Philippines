Region 10 - Lanao Del Norte Itinerary

Itinerary 1: 

Day 1:

Morning: Start your exploration of Lanao del Norte by visiting the Maria Cristina Falls in Iligan City. Marvel at the majestic twin waterfalls and witness the power of the cascading water. Enjoy the beautiful surroundings and take memorable photos of the falls.

Afternoon: Head to the Tinago Falls in Linamon. Embark on a short trek to reach the falls hidden within a deep ravine. Take a refreshing dip in the crystal-clear waters and admire the natural beauty of the surroundings.

Evening: Experience the vibrant night scene at Timoga Springs in Iligan City. Relax and soak in the natural spring pools surrounded by lush vegetation. Enjoy the cool waters and unwind after a day of exploration.

Day 2:

Morning: Take a trip to the Ma. Cristina Ancestral House in Iligan City. Explore the well-preserved heritage house and learn about the history and culture of the area. Admire the antique furniture, artifacts, and photographs showcasing the rich heritage of Lanao del Norte.

Afternoon: Visit the Mount Agad-Agad in Iligan City. Embark on a trekking adventure to reach the summit and be rewarded with panoramic views of the city and its surroundings. Enjoy the fresh mountain air and take in the stunning vistas.

Evening: Dine at a local restaurant in Iligan City and savor the local cuisine. Sample traditional dishes such as lechon de Iligan (roasted pig), kinilaw (ceviche), and tinolang manok (chicken ginger soup). Experience the flavors of Lanao del Norte's culinary offerings.

Day 3:

Morning: Explore the Mimbalot Falls in Brgy. Buru-un, Iligan City. Take a short hike to reach the falls and enjoy its beauty and tranquility. Take a refreshing swim in the natural pool beneath the waterfall and relax amidst the lush surroundings.

Afternoon: Visit the Iligan Anahaw Amphitheater. Enjoy the cultural performances showcasing traditional dances and music of the local communities. Immerse yourself in the vibrant cultural heritage of Lanao del Norte.

Evening: Take a leisurely walk along the promenade at Maria Cristina Baywalk in Iligan City. Enjoy the view of the sunset over the Iligan Bay and feel the cool sea breeze. Take a moment to reflect on your time in Lanao del Norte and appreciate the natural beauty of the region.
