Region 12 - Cotabato Itinerary

Itinerary 1: 

Day 1:

Morning: Start your exploration of Cotabato City by visiting the Grand Mosque, also known as the Sultan Haji Hassanal Bolkiah Masjid. Marvel at the majestic architecture and intricate designs of this Islamic place of worship. Take a guided tour to learn about the Islamic faith and the significance of the mosque in the community.

Afternoon: Explore the Tamontaka Church, also known as the Cotabato Cathedral. Admire the beautiful architecture and stained glass windows of this historic church. Take a moment for reflection and appreciate the spiritual atmosphere of the cathedral.

Evening: Visit the Kutawato Cave and Spring in Barangay Awang. Explore the cave system and enjoy the refreshing spring waters. Take a leisurely walk around the area and enjoy the natural surroundings. You can also have a picnic near the spring.

Day 2:

Morning: Take a trip to the Rio Grande de Mindanao in Sultan Kudarat, approximately 1-2 hours from Cotabato City. Enjoy a scenic boat ride along the river, surrounded by lush greenery and beautiful landscapes. Experience the tranquility of the river and spot various bird species and wildlife along the way.

Afternoon: Visit the Old Cotabato City Hall, also known as the Kutawato Caves and Tinago Spring. Explore the historical caves and learn about their significance in the local culture and history. Take a dip in the refreshing Tinago Spring and enjoy the serene atmosphere.

Evening: Experience a traditional Maguindanaon performance at one of the cultural centers in Cotabato City. Enjoy vibrant dances, traditional music, and colorful costumes. Engage with the performers and learn more about the rich cultural heritage of the Maguindanaon people.

Day 3:

Morning: Visit the Sultan Kudarat Provincial Capitol Complex in Isulan, Sultan Kudarat, approximately 1-2 hours from Cotabato City. Explore the complex and learn about the local government and the history of the province. Take a leisurely walk around the area and enjoy the architectural beauty of the buildings.

Afternoon: Take a trip to the nearby town of Midsayap and visit the Libungan Tunnels. Explore the underground tunnels that were used during World War II. Learn about their historical significance and the stories behind them.

Evening: Wrap up your visit to Cotabato City by enjoying a relaxing evening in one of the local restaurants. Sample traditional Maguindanaon dishes and savor the flavors of the region's cuisine. Reflect on your experiences in Cotabato City and appreciate the cultural diversity of the region.
