BARMM - Cotabato Itinerary

Itinerary 1: 

Day 1:

Morning: Start your day by visiting the Kutawato Cave in Cotabato City. Explore the natural limestone cave formations and learn about their geological significance. Take a guided tour to discover the hidden chambers and unique rock formations inside the cave.

Afternoon: Explore the Tamontaka Church in Cotabato City. Visit this historic church, also known as the Our Lady of Lourdes Parish Church, which dates back to the Spanish colonial period. Learn about its architectural significance and its role in the history of Christianity in Cotabato.

Evening: Experience a traditional Maguindanao cultural performance. Witness the Maguindanao people showcase their traditional dances, music, and attire, which reflect their rich cultural heritage. Engage in cultural exchanges and learn about their way of life.

Day 2:

Morning:  Take a trip to the stunning Asik-Asik Falls in Alamada, Cotabato. Enjoy a scenic trek through the lush forest to reach the falls. Marvel at the curtain-like waterfalls cascading down the rock formations. Take a refreshing dip in the natural pools and soak in the beauty of this hidden gem.

Afternoon: Explore the Grand Mosque of Cotabato, also known as the Sultan Haji Hassanal Bolkiah Mosque. Admire the grandeur of this mosque, which is one of the largest in the Philippines. Learn about Islamic architecture and its significance in the Muslim community.

Evening: Enjoy a traditional Maguindanaoan feast featuring local delicacies and specialties. Experience the flavors of Maguindanao cuisine, known for its rich spices and unique combinations of flavors.

Day 3:

Morning: Visit the Shariff Kabunsuan Cultural Complex in Cotabato City. Immerse yourself in the rich cultural heritage of the Maguindanao people as you visit the cultural museum and witness traditional arts and crafts demonstrations. Learn about the history, traditions, and customs of the local community.

Afternoon: Explore the People's Palace in Cotabato City. Visit the provincial capitol complex and learn about the local government and governance in the region. Enjoy the beautiful gardens and architectural design of the complex.

Evening: Take a leisurely stroll along the Tantawan Park in Cotabato City. Enjoy the view of the Rio Grande de Mindanao River, take in the colorful sunset, and experience the vibrant atmosphere of the park. Check other Itinerary
