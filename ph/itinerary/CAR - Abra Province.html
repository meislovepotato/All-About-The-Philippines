CAR - Abra Itinerary

Itinerary 1: Nature and Cultural Exploration

Day 1:

Morning: Start your day by visiting the Kaparkan Falls in Tineg. Enjoy the scenic beauty of the waterfall and take a refreshing dip in its cool waters. Immerse yourself in the natural surroundings and capture memorable photos.

Afternoon: Explore the town of Bangued, the capital of Abra Province. Visit the St. James the Elder Cathedral, a historic church known for its intricate architecture and religious significance. Take a leisurely walk along the Calaba Bridge and enjoy the views of the Abra River.

Evening: Attend a cultural performance showcasing the traditional dances and music of the Tingguians, one of the indigenous groups in Abra. Experience the vibrant costumes and rhythmic beats that depict their cultural heritage.

Day 2:

Morning: Visit the Victoria Park in Bangued. Enjoy the peaceful ambiance of the park, stroll through its gardens, and appreciate the beautiful views of the surrounding mountains. Take a moment to relax and unwind in this serene setting.

Afternoon: Explore the centuries-old Kapitan Berong House in Tayum. Learn about the history and architecture of this ancestral house, which serves as a museum showcasing the cultural heritage of the region.

Evening: Engage in a cultural exchange with the local communities in Abra. Visit a traditional weaving center and observe the intricate art of weaving by local artisans. Learn about the significance of traditional textiles in Abra's culture.

Day 3:

Morning: Embark on a scenic hike to the Kili Falls in Dolores. Enjoy the picturesque landscapes along the trail and marvel at the cascading waterfalls. Take in the tranquility of the natural surroundings and enjoy a refreshing swim.

Afternoon: Visit the Lusuac Spring in Lagangilang. Take a dip in the crystal-clear spring waters and enjoy the peaceful ambiance of the area. Take leisurely walks along the spring's banks and appreciate the beauty of the surrounding nature.

Evening: Indulge in a traditional Abra feast featuring local delicacies and specialties. Savor the unique flavors of Abra's cuisine and experience the warm hospitality of the locals.
