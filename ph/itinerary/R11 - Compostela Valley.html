Region 11 - Compostela Itinerary

Itinerary 1: 

Day 1:

Morning: Start your exploration of Compostela Valley by visiting the Aliwagwag Falls in Cateel. Marvel at the stunning cascades of this multi-tiered waterfall, often called the "Stairway to Heaven." Enjoy swimming in the cool, clear waters and take in the breathtaking natural beauty of the surrounding rainforest.

Afternoon: Head to the Maragusan Hot and Cold Spring Resort in Maragusan. Relax in the natural hot springs and rejuvenate your senses. Enjoy a dip in the cool spring waters or take a relaxing soak in the warm pools amidst a lush tropical setting.

Evening: Visit the Nabunturan Public Market in Nabunturan. Explore the market and sample local delicacies and fresh produce. Immerse yourself in the local culture, interact with the friendly locals, and discover unique products from the region.

Day 2:

Morning: Take a trip to the Mount Diwata Range in Monkayo. Embark on a hiking adventure to Mount Diwata and explore the scenic landscapes and rich biodiversity. Enjoy the panoramic views and capture memorable photos of the surrounding mountain range.

Afternoon: Visit the Maco Banana Plantation in the municipality of Maco. Take a guided tour of the plantation and learn about the cultivation and processing of bananas. Gain insights into the region's significant role in the country's banana industry.

Evening: Enjoy a relaxing evening at Lake Leonard in New Bataan. Take a boat ride on the tranquil lake, surrounded by lush greenery and towering mountains. Enjoy the peaceful ambiance and take in the natural beauty of the area.

Day 3:

Morning: Explore the Mainit Hot Spring in Nabunturan. Take a dip in the soothing hot spring waters and relax amidst the natural surroundings. Enjoy the therapeutic benefits of the mineral-rich waters and soak up the tranquility of this hidden gem.

Afternoon: Visit the Compostela Valley Provincial Capitol in Nabunturan. Take a guided tour to learn about the local government and the history of the province. Explore the park surrounding the capitol and enjoy the serene ambiance.

Evening: Wrap up your visit to Compostela Valley by enjoying a dinner at a local restaurant in Nabunturan. Indulge in delicious Filipino dishes and savor the flavors of the region. Reflect on your time in Compostela Valley and appreciate the natural wonders and cultural richness of the area.
