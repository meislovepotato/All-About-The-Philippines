CAR - Baguio Itinerary

Itinerary 1: 

Day 1:

Morning: Start your day by exploring the famous Baguio City Market. Experience the vibrant atmosphere as you browse through various stalls selling fresh produce, local crafts, and souvenirs. Don't forget to try some local snacks like strawberry taho and Baguio longganisa.

Afternoon: Visit the Baguio Botanical Garden. Take a leisurely stroll through the lush gardens and admire the diverse collection of plants and flowers. Learn about the indigenous culture of the Cordillera region through the traditional huts and sculptures found within the garden.

Evening: Enjoy a scenic sunset view from Mines View Park. Take in the panoramic vista of the surrounding mountains, valleys, and Baguio City itself. Don't miss the opportunity to have your photo taken wearing traditional Cordillera attire.

Day 2:

Morning: Start your day with a visit to the iconic Burnham Park. Rent a paddleboat and leisurely cruise around the man-made lake. Take a relaxing walk or bike ride along the park's pathways, and don't forget to try some local snacks and delicacies from the food stalls.

Afternoon: Head to the Camp John Hay Historical Core. Explore the historical buildings, gardens, and take a guided tour to learn about the rich history of the area. Enjoy the cool climate and serene surroundings of this former rest and recreation facility.

Evening: Indulge in a warm cup of locally grown coffee at one of Baguio's famous coffee shops. Relax and enjoy the cozy ambiance while savoring the unique flavors of Benguet coffee.

Day 3:

Morning: Embark on a visit to the BenCab Museum. Admire the artworks of national artist Benedicto Cabrera (BenCab) and other contemporary artists. Explore the different galleries, including the museum's lush garden and view deck that offers a stunning view of the mountains.

Afternoon: Take a scenic drive up to the Strawberry Farm in La Trinidad, just outside Baguio City. Enjoy strawberry picking and savor the experience of harvesting your own fresh strawberries. You can also indulge in strawberry-inspired treats and delicacies.

Evening: Visit the Baguio Cathedral (Our Lady of the Atonement Cathedral). Attend a mass or simply soak in the spiritual atmosphere of this beautiful church. Take a moment for reflection and appreciate the stunning architecture.
