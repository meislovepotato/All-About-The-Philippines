CAR - Mountain Province Itinerary

Itinerary 1: Nature and Cultural Exploration

Day 1:

Morning: Start your day by visiting the Bontoc Museum in Bontoc town. Learn about the rich cultural heritage of the indigenous people of Mountain Province, including their traditional practices, artifacts, and rituals.

Afternoon: Take a scenic drive to Sagada. Explore the famous Sumaguing Cave, known for its impressive rock formations and underground river. Engage in an exhilarating spelunking adventure with the assistance of local guides.

Evening: Enjoy a relaxing bonfire and storytelling session at your accommodation in Sagada. Immerse yourself in the local folklore and legends of the region.

Day 2:

Morning: Wake up early to witness the breathtaking sunrise at Kiltepan Viewpoint. Marvel at the panoramic view of the surrounding mountains and rice terraces as the sun paints the sky with vibrant hues.

Afternoon: Go on a trek to the Bomod-Ok Falls. Enjoy the scenic hike through terraced rice fields and immerse yourself in the natural beauty of the cascading waterfall. Take a refreshing dip in the cool waters.

Evening: Experience the unique burial traditions of Sagada by visiting the hanging coffins. Gain insights into the local customs and beliefs surrounding death and the afterlife.

Itinerary 2: 

Day 1:

Morning: Embark on a challenging hike to Mount Pulag, the third-highest peak in the Philippines. Enjoy the breathtaking views of the sea of clouds and surrounding mountains from the summit. Please note that this hike requires prior arrangements and a local guide.

Afternoon: Visit the Echo Valley and Hanging Coffins in Sagada. Take a leisurely stroll through the lush valley, known for its echoing sounds, and observe the ancient hanging coffins suspended on the cliffs.

Evening: Indulge in a traditional Igorot dinner featuring local specialties such as pinikpikan (chicken dish) and etag (cured meat). Immerse yourself in the flavors and cultural heritage of the region.
