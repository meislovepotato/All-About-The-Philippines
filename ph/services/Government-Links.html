Cabinet Departments
  1. Department of Agriculture (DA) - Responsible for the development and promotion of the agricultural sector.
  2. Department of Budget and Management (DBM) - Formulates the national budget and ensures efficient allocation of resources.
  3. Department of Education (DepEd) - Oversees the country's basic education system.
  4. Department of Energy (DOE) - Responsible for the exploration, development, and utilization of energy resources.
  5. Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) - Protects and manages the environment and natural resources.
  6. Department of Finance (DOF) - Manages the country's finances, revenue collection, and fiscal policies.
  7. Department of Foreign Affairs (DFA) - Handles the country's foreign relations and diplomatic affairs.
  8. Department of Health (DOH) - Promotes and protects the health of Filipinos.
  9. Department of the Interior and Local Government (DILG) - Oversees local governance and law enforcement.
  10. Department of Justice (DOJ) - Ensures the rule of law and administers justice.
  11. Department of Labor and Employment (DOLE) - Safeguards workers' rights and promotes employment opportunities.
  12. Department of National Defense (DND) - Ensures national security and defense.
  13. Department of Public Works and Highways (DPWH) - Develops and maintains infrastructure projects.
  14. Department of Science and Technology (DOST) - Promotes scientific and technological advancements.
  15. Department of Social Welfare and Development (DSWD) - Provides social welfare programs and services.
  16. Department of Tourism (DOT) - Promotes and develops the tourism industry in the Philippines.
  17. Department of Trade and Industry (DTI) - Promotes trade, investments, and consumer protection.
  18. Department of Transportation (DOTr) - Regulates and develops transportation systems in the country.
Independent Agencies
  1. Commission on Audit (COA) - Examines and audits government accounts.
  2. Commission on Elections (COMELEC) - Oversees and conducts elections in the Philippines.
  3. Commission on Human Rights (CHR) - Protects and promotes human rights in the country.
  4. Civil Service Commission (CSC) - Manages the civil service and ensures government workforce efficiency.
  5. National Economic and Development Authority (NEDA) - Formulates development plans and policies.
  6. National Commission for Culture and the Arts (NCCA) - Preserves and promotes Philippine arts and culture.
  7. Philippine National Police (PNP) - Maintains peace and order and enforces the law.
  8. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) - Regulates the securities market and protects investors.
  9. Social Security System (SSS) - Provides social security benefits and services to members.
Legislative Branch
  1. Senate of the Philippines - The upper chamber of the Philippine Congress.
  2. House of Representatives - The lower chamber of the Philippine Congress.
Judicial Branch
  1. Supreme Court of the Philippines - The highest judicial body in the country.
Financial Institutions and Regulatory Bodies
  1. Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas (BSP) - The central bank of the Philippines responsible for monetary policy and financial stability.
  2. Insurance Commission (IC) - Regulates the insurance industry in the Philippines.
  3. Philippine Deposit Insurance Corporation (PDIC) - Provides deposit insurance coverage for banks.
Health and Social Services
  1. Philippine Health Insurance Corporation (PhilHealth) - Provides health insurance coverage to Filipinos.
  2. Food and Drug Administration (FDA) - Ensures the safety and quality of food, drugs, and other healthcare products.
  3. National Nutrition Council (NNC) - Promotes nutrition and healthy eating in the country.
  4. Philippine Red Cross (PRC) - Provides humanitarian and emergency services.
Education and Research Institutions
  1. Commission on Higher Education (CHED) - Oversees higher education institutions and formulates policies.
  2. Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA) - Provides technical-vocational education and training programs.
  3. Philippine Normal University (PNU) - A public research university focused on teacher education.
Infrastructure and Utilities
  1. National Grid Corporation of the Philippines (NGCP) - Manages and operates the country's power transmission network.
  2. Metropolitan Waterworks and Sewerage System (MWSS) - Regulates and oversees water supply and wastewater services in Metro Manila.
  3. Philippine Ports Authority (PPA) - Manages and operates ports and harbors in the country.
  4. National Housing Authority (NHA) - Develops and implements housing programs for low-income families.
Transportation and Infrastructure
  1. Department of Transportation (DOTr) - Responsible for the planning, implementation, and regulation of transportation systems.
  2. Land Transportation Office (LTO) - Registers and licenses motor vehicles and drivers.
  3. Civil Aviation Authority of the Philippines (CAAP) - Regulates civil aviation and operates airports in the country.
  4. Philippine National Railways (PNR) - Operates the main railway system in Luzon.
Environmental Protection and Natural Resources
  1. Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) - Oversees the management and conservation of the environment and natural resources.
  2. Environmental Management Bureau (EMB) - Implements environmental policies and regulations.
  3. Mines and Geosciences Bureau (MGB) - Regulates the exploration and extraction of mineral resources.
  4. Biodiversity Management Bureau (BMB) - Promotes the conservation and sustainable use of biodiversity.
Tourism and Culture
  1. Department of Tourism (DOT) - Promotes and develops the tourism industry in the Philippines.
  2. National Commission for Culture and the Arts (NCCA) - Preserves and promotes Philippine culture and arts.
  3. Intramuros Administration - Manages and preserves the historic walled city of Intramuros in Manila.
  4. Tourism Infrastructure and Enterprise Zone Authority (TIEZA) - Develops tourism infrastructure and manages tourism enterprise zones.
Science and Technology
  1. Department of Science and Technology (DOST) - Promotes scientific research, development, and innovation.
  2. Philippine Atmospheric, Geophysical, and Astronomical Services Administration (PAGASA) - Provides weather forecasting and related services.
  3. Philippine Council for Agriculture, Aquatic and Natural Resources Research and Development (PCAARRD) - Coordinates and funds research in agriculture, aquatic resources, and natural resources.
  4. Science Education Institute (SEI) - Provides scholarships and support for science and technology education.
